Introduction

Introduction

Goal

Goal

Think about your resources as data           (from time to time)

Benefits
  1. you can manage them more flexibly
  2. you can make them easier for other researchers to use
YOU will be able to manage them more flexibly (search, sort, combine etc.) and to use additional methods of analysis and you will make it easier FOR OTHER RESEARCHERS to not just consume your offerings, but to put them to productive use for their own purposes.

Data Modelling

Data Modelling

Nature of your resource

  • Do you find or do you create this information?
  • Is it about concepts, about objects or about other information?
  • Are there taxonomies, relations to other resources/data?
  • Which bits are relevant to you?

Your data structures must reflect this!
